<p><p>The 5-axis water-jet cutter market is primarily divided into two types: pure water-jet cutters and abrasive water-jet cutters. Pure water-jet cutters utilize high-pressure water streams to cut soft materials like foam and cardboard, providing precise edges without material degradation. In contrast, abrasive water-jet cutters mix water with abrasive particles, allowing for effective cutting of harder materials like metals and stone. Both types enhance versatility in manufacturing and fabrication, catering to diverse industries by offering precision and efficient material removal.</p></p>
