<p><strong>The Portable Radiation Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas Ionization Detectors</li><li>Semiconductor Detectors</li><li>Scintillation Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable radiation detectors market comprises three key types: gas ionization detectors, semiconductor detectors, and scintillation detectors. Gas ionization detectors utilize gases to ionize radiation, producing measurable electrical currents. Semiconductor detectors rely on solid-state materials, offering high resolution and efficiency in detecting ionizing radiation. Scintillation detectors employ scintillating materials that emit light when exposed to radiation, enabling the conversion of detected signals into visible light for analysis. Each type serves distinct applications in fields like safety, medical, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
