<p><p>The solar reflective window films market comprises mainly two types: reflective films and dual reflective films. Reflective films are designed to reduce glare and control heat by reflecting sunlight, making indoor environments more comfortable and energy-efficient. Dual reflective films, on the other hand, offer a balance of external reflectivity and internal visibility, ensuring privacy during the day while still allowing natural light to enter. Both types contribute to energy savings, enhancing building aesthetics and occupant comfort.</p></p>
