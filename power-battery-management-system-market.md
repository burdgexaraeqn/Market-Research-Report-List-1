<p><p>The Power Battery Management System market is categorized into three types: Distributed, Centralized, and Modular. </p><p>Distributed systems manage battery units independently across various locations, enhancing reliability and scalability. Centralized systems consolidate battery management in a single unit, which simplifies oversight and reduces costs but may create a single point of failure. Modular systems provide flexible configurations that allow for easy upgrading or replacement of battery units, optimizing performance and customization based on specific application needs. Each type addresses unique operational requirements.</p></p>
