<p><strong>The OPGW (Optical Ground Wire) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Central Tube Structure OPGW</li><li>Layer Stranding Structure OPGW</li></ul></p>
<p><p>OPGW (Optical Ground Wire) serves dual purposes as a grounding wire and an optical fiber communication medium in power transmission lines. The Central Tube Structure OPGW features a central tube housing optical fibers, offering enhanced protection and efficiency. In contrast, the Layer Stranding Structure OPGW consists of multiple layers of optical fibers wound around a steel core, allowing for greater flexibility and ease of installation. Both types cater to different transmission needs while ensuring reliable communication and safety in energy networks.</p></p>
