<p><p>The aircraft inertial systems market includes various types designed for navigation and attitude determination. AHRS (Attitude and Heading Reference Systems) provide orientation data. INS (Inertial Navigation Systems) offer precise location tracking based on inertial measurements. IMU (Inertial Measurement Units) combine accelerometers and gyroscopes for motion tracking. Laser inertial systems use laser technology for high-precision navigation. Other types may encompass hybrid systems or advanced technologies, addressing specific needs in the aviation industry, enhancing safety and operational efficiency.</p></p>
