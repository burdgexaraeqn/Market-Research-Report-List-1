<p><p>The premium car tires market consists primarily of bias tires and radial tires, each catering to different performance and driving needs. Bias tires have crisscrossing layers that provide enhanced durability and resistance to punctures, making them suitable for certain off-road and vintage vehicles. In contrast, radial tires feature layers arranged radially from the center, offering superior grip, improved fuel efficiency, and better handling on highways. These distinctions influence consumer preferences based on vehicle type, driving conditions, and desired performance characteristics.</p></p>
