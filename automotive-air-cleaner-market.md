<p><p>The automotive air cleaner market consists of two primary types: dry type and wet type air cleaners. Dry type air cleaners utilize a filter made of paper or foam to capture dust and debris without the need for maintenance beyond replacement. In contrast, wet type air cleaners use an oil-soaked filter to trap contaminants, requiring regular upkeep but providing superior filtration, especially in harsh environments. Both types serve the crucial role of ensuring optimal engine performance and longevity by maintaining clean air intake.</p></p>
